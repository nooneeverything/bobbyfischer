Legacy

Bobby Fischer is still to this day considered one of the best chess players in history. His games even to this day are studied by chess enthusiasts all over the world. He is considered a important part of chess being integrated into mainstream culture. His victory in 1972 not only showed the world the value of chess but cemented him in the history of the game forever. Even though his personal life was fulled with controversy and scandle those of us who study the game and its culture cant help but seeing Fischer as a shining example of how the game is supposed to be played, with wisdom and grace.

"Chess is life" - Bobby Fischer
