Early Life



Bobby Fischer was born in Chicago Illinois on March 9th, 1943. His mother, Regina Wender Fischer, was born in Switzerland and was a US citizen. She was of Polish decent. Regina throughout her life was a teacher, a nurse, and a physician. Regina and Bobbys father divorced when Bobby was a toddler. At the time of Bobbys birth. His journey in chess began at age 6 when his sister bought a chess set from a candy store. They taught themselves using the instructions from the set. After his sister lost interest, young Fischer became engulfed in the game. So much so his mother began to worry about the amount of time he was spending alone playing himself. She even went so fas as to put a postcard to the Brooklyn Eagle newspaper seeking to place an ad inquiring if there were any other children who would like to play with Bobby. Chess caused a strain between Fischer in his mother who preferred he had other interests besides chess.
